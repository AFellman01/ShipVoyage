Getting you what you need, when you need it.

The code as written accepts USPS numbers, DHL tracking numbers, UPS tracking numbers, and FedEx numbers. USPS numbers come in two formats. The first is thirteen digits long, and starts with an E. The second is twenty-two digits long, and starts with a 9. UPS tracking numbers are eighteen digits long and start with the characters 1Z. FedEx tracking numbers are usually twelve digits long, and DHL tracking numbers are ten digits long. Neither contain any letters.
